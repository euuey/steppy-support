Account Deletion Request

You can request the permanent deletion of your Steppy account and all associated data at any time.

✉️ How to Request Deletion

Send an email to our support team using the button below. To verify ownership of the account, please include the Google account email address you used to sign in to Steppy.

Include in your email:
The Google account email address associated with your Steppy account (e.g., yourname@gmail.com).
Request Account Deletion

🗑️ What Will Be Deleted

Upon confirmation of your deletion request, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account profile and authentication information
  • All game progress, step records, and activity history
  • In-app preferences and settings
  • Any other personal data associated with your account

📋 What May Be Retained Temporary

In limited circumstances, certain data may be retained for a short period after deletion for the following purposes:

  • Compliance with applicable laws or regulations
  • Security incident investigation and fraud prevention
  • Resolving outstanding disputes or enforcing our terms
  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics (not linked to your identity)

Any retained data will be deleted as soon as the applicable retention period expires.

⏱️ Processing Time

We will process your account deletion request within 7 business days of receiving your email. You will receive a confirmation once the deletion is complete.
